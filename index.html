About Me

Born in the great white North, my jouney began with humble beginings in the town of Kamloops, Biritsh Colombia. At the age of 4, my family moved to Denver Colorado. 10 years later my family once again moved to Washington State, where I attended Issaquah High School, just outside Seattle. During high school I joined the swim team, where I was captain my senior year, I was also involved in student government.

After high school, I moved back to Colorado where I attended the University of Colorado Boulder
